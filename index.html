The Network of Ulster Cycle Characters

The graph shows the co-occurrence of characters in the Ulster cycle (31 text from this corpus). Every circle (node) corresponds to a character: the bigger it is, the more frequently a character is mentioned. If two characters are connected with a link, they occur together in a window of size 10. The thicker the link, the more often the characters co-occur.
